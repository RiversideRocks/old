NOTICE TO YOUTUBE CREATORS! FTC and COPPA crack down on YouTube!
11/13/19

Recently, YouTube resolved a dispute about protecting children online with the United States Federal Trade Commission.
YouTube was accused of taking data on minors for personalised ads. From now on, YouTube will no longer support personalised ads,
notifications, comments and more on video that are labeled for children in Creator Studio or by an AI developed by Youtube.
For more about this, please visit YouTube's support page about this.
If you are a YouTube Creator, please visit YouTube Studio.
